McLaren F1 Team


McLaren F1 Facts

  • McLaren's last Driver's Championship was won in 2008 by Lewis Hamilton.
  • McLaren's last Constructor's Championship was won in 1998 with Mika Hakkinen and David Coulthard.
  • Daniel Ricciardo gave McLaren their most recent win at the 2021 Italian Grand Prix.
  • Ayrton Senna has the most wins out of any McLaren F1 driver with 35 wins.
  • McLaren was the first team to race with a fully carbon fiber composite chassis, which is now universal on the grid.
  • David Coulthard holds the most race starts for McLaren with 150 grand prix starts.
  • Team was founded by Bruce McLaren who raced under his own team.
  • McLaren fields a new driver for the 2023 season with Oscar Piastri replacing Daniel Ricciardo.
